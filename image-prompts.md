# Renova Trade Website Image Prompts

Use these prompts with AI image generators (Midjourney, DALL-E, Stable Diffusion, etc.) to create realistic images for the website.

- **Banner/hero backgrounds**: 1920×1080 px (16:9)
- **Service/product card images**: 800×600 px (4:3)
- **About section image**: 800×1000 px (4:5)
- **CTA background**: 1920×600 px (wide)

## Style guidelines
- Keep images clean, professional, and industrial.
- Use natural or golden-hour lighting where possible.
- Avoid cluttered or overly dramatic compositions.
- Maintain a consistent color palette: blues, greys, oranges, and metallic tones.
- Use photorealistic style, not illustrations.
